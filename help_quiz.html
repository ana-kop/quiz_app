User Guide to Quiz Answering

Answering questions 

1. Once you load the app you will see markers with different questions; these markers will be orange by default and will only show questions you have not answered correctly (on your first try), or have not answered at all. 

2. You can answer questions in the following way: 

     A) When you are nearby, a question will open*, and you can select one of the 4 options and click "Submit Answer". 

     B) You can click on a quiz marker anywhere else on the quiz to answer a question, select one of the 4 options on the pop up form and click "Submit Answer". 

3. You will then be told whether your answer is correct, and the marker will turn green if it is; you will also be told how many questions you have answered correctly so far and your overall ranking. 


*IMPORTANT: please be aware that there may be a small delay in location tracking and it may take several seconds for the question to open automatically when you are on the move!



Additional functionality (accessible from the side menu)

  • Click "Show exisiting questions" -> "5 closest questions" and pink markers will appear, showing 5 questions that are currently closest to your location. To remove these questions from the view, click "Remove exisiting questions" -> "5 closest questions"
  • Click "Show exisiting questions" -> "Last 5 questions answered" and markers will show the 5 questions you last answered, colour coded based on whether the answer was right or wrong. To remove these questions from the view, click "Remove exisiting questions" -> "Last 5 questions answered"
  • Click "Top 5 Scorers" to see the 5 users who answered the most questions correctly.
